Character 1 The Narrator

A prisoner who just got out of jail stays in an abandoned building in Barcelona. He has in a romantic relationship with Laura, but after learning the truth about her, his life takes a turn for the worse.

Character 2 Laura

A product of the narrator's imagination. Laura is an enigmatic young woman who works for the building’s landlord and has a mysterious connection to the place. She died during the Spanish Civil War, linking her death to the building's history.

Character 3 Doña Luisa

Another creation of the narrator's mind, Doña Luisa is a compassionate neighbor who offers him meals, symbolizing the comfort and familiarity of family and home.

Character 4 Don Florian

A fellow imaginative presence in the narrator's life, Don Florian is a kind, elderly neighbor and a creative who spends his time painting dolls and toys.

Character 5 Cellmates

A disruptive group of individuals who frequently mock the narrator, contributing to his alienation and despair.

Character 6 Hernán

a librarian who uncovers a ten-year-old newspaper article about a bombing during the Civil War, providing crucial historical context that connects to the narrator’s experiences.
